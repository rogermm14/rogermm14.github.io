I am a computer vision research engineer specialized in 3D vision tasks (reconstruction, calibration, co-registration, change detection, etc.). I am currenty a post-doc at the Centre Borelli at ENS Paris-Saclay, near Paris. My current research topic is the application of neural rendering methods to satellite image collections.

I am Catalan, born in Barcelona (1995), former student of Universitat Pompeu Fabra. In Barcelona I completed a BSc in Audiovisual Systems Engineering and then a specialized MSc in Computer Vision. I moved to Paris in October 2018 to do my PhD in 3D modeling from satellite imagery, under the supervision of Gabriele Facciolo, at the Centre Borelli. I defended my thesis Applications of multi-image remote sensing in December 2022.

‪Roger Marí‬ Molas

Computer Vision Research Engineer (PhD) with a strong background in image processing and deep learning. My doctoral research focused on remote sensing and 3D vision tasks (reconstruction, calibration, co-registration, change detection). More recently, my work has centered on neural rendering for remote-sensing imagery, cultural heritage digitization, and the development and application of generative AI methods.

Born in Barcelona (1995), I studied at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, where I earned a BSc with honors in Audiovisual Systems Engineering and a specialized MSc in Computer Vision. I moved to Paris in October 2018 to pursue a PhD at Centre Borelli (ENS Paris-Saclay) under the supervision of Gabriele Facciolo. I defended my thesis Applications of multi-image remote sensing in December 2022. In January 2024, I joined the Multimedia Technologies Unit at Eurecat, where I currently contribute to national and European projects in Computer Vision and AI.
